The Golden Retriever

America's Favorite Family Dog

The Golden Retriever has long been deemed America's favorite family dog. With their charming appearance and loving personality, it's impossible to not instantly fall in love. You are surely to make them your best friend when you see how far their loyalty goes. No wonder the Golden Retriever has been the #1 choice for families across the nation! There will never be any dull moments with this loving creature around. Make sure you are prepared for lots of play time and are geared with treats when welcoming your fur buddy to your home! 🐕
